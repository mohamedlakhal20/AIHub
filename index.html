Eleven Labs:

is an AI-powered platform for generating high-quality voice using text-to-speech technology. It provides tools to create realistic human-like voices, used in various applications such as podcasts, audiobooks, and games. It is known for its ability to accurately mimic different accents and tones

Murf:

Murf is a popular platform for creating voiceovers, especially for video content. It offers over 120 voices in 20+ languages and includes advanced editing features like adjusting pitch and speed.

Explore
Google Cloud Text-to-Speech:

Google's service provides over 380 human-quality voices across multiple languages. It allows for customization of pitch and tone and is suitable for those looking to scale content globally

Explore
Natural Reader:

A simple, user-friendly tool, Natural Reader allows you to convert text into speech quickly. It supports multiple file formats and offers both free and paid plans, with over 100 voices on the paid plans.

To convert text to image

I recommend the following sites:

Deep Dream Generator:

This site uses Google's Deep Dream algorithm to transform text into stunning, dream-like images. You can upload an image or start from scratch, and the AI will generate visuals based on the text you input. It's great for creating surreal and artistic images.

Explore
Artbreeder:

This platform allows users to create and explore images through the power of collaborative AI. You can type in text to guide the creation process and then tweak and blend different images to get the perfect result. It's a fantastic tool for generating and evolving art.

Explore
DALL-E Mini:

Inspired by OpenAI's DALL-E, this mini version lets you enter text prompts and generates images based on your descriptions. It's a fun way to see how A interprets and visualizes different concepts, though it might not always get everything perfect!

Explore
NightCafe:

This AI art generator offers multiple algorithms to convert text into images, including styles like neural style transfer and VQGAN+CLIP. You can customize the settings to get the exact look you want, making it a versatile tool for generating beautiful and unique visuals.
